Welcome to Minimalist.

Feel free to reach out for a free consultation, customer references, or other information.

Minimalist is a small, tightly knit outfit, headquartered in Santa Monica, CA. Kurt Koller is our founder, guru, lead systems architect and programming manager. We have a talented group of programmers both local and abroad, and we collaborate on projects with the best designers and information architects around.

Despite our small size, Minimalist has taken on projects for big clients like Merck, Pfizer, Astra Zeneca, Microsoft, AOL, Area/Code (Zynga NYC), A&E TV Networks, Growing Family Inc., Ziff Davis Media, Bank of America, Local Projects, and many more, developing websites, games, experiential promotional events/technology, and mobile applications.

Minimalist was born in 1997, when after fifteen years as a successful software engineer, Kurt decided he could be more effective and build better code by developing a new approach to the craft of programming. Our goal, simply put, is to do it right the first time.
